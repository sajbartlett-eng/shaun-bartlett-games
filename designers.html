Part 2: The Methodology Gap

Why most educational design fails to engage.

üö´ The Entertainment Trap

The Conflict: Artificial balance kills the lesson.

Solution: Lean into unfairness. The fun comes from the primal struggle against a rigged system.

üö´ The Virtue Trap

The Conflict: Preaching triggers defensiveness.

Solution: Neutrality. When the game doesn't judge, the player judges themselves.

üö´ The Fidelity Trap

The Conflict: Complexity blocks access.

Solution: Folk Mechanics. Use tools people already know (cards, dice, words).

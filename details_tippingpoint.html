THE TIPPING POINT

Climate Crisis as Push-Your-Luck

3-6 Players 15-30 Minutes Age 14+ Digital

You're a CEO. Every turn, you invest in one of three sectors. Deregulation pays the most but pushes the planet toward collapse. Green tech pays the least but saves everyone. Can you profit without triggering extinction?

The Three Investment Sectors

DEREGULATION (Roll ≤ Market Value)

Highest profit ($5-7 depending on role) but adds +1-2 Crisis points. Once Crisis ≥5, deregulation adds +2 instead of +1. The system accelerates.

CONVENTIONAL (Roll Between Dereg-Green Range)

Moderate profit ($3-5), adds +1 Crisis. Business as usual.

GREEN TECH (Roll ≥ Market Value)

Lowest profit ($2-4) but adds +1 Environment Health. Win condition: reach Environment 5.

The Race Against Collapse

Win: Environment reaches 5 (planet saved)

Lose: Crisis reaches 10-14 depending on difficulty (planetary collapse)

Secured Investments

You don't just roll dice and hope. Players "secure" their investments by paying more:

  • $1 - Unsecured: Pure dice roll
  • $2 - Light: ±1 to target range
  • $3 - Moderate: ±2 to target range
  • $4 - Heavy: ±3 to target range

This creates risk/reward decisions: cheap and risky vs. expensive and safe.

The Finance Player

One player has a completely different role—pure speculation:

  • Bet on a Sector: Predict what others will choose, profit if correct
  • Offer Loans: Give $1, receive $2 if they succeed (predatory lending)

Finance profits from others' risk without directly affecting Crisis or Environment.

Why It Works

The game embodies the tragedy of the commons. Individual rational choices (maximize profit) create collective catastrophe (planetary collapse). Players experience:

  • Why climate action fails when profit drives decisions
  • How positive feedback loops accelerate crisis
  • The gap between individual rationality and collective survival
  • Path dependency—early choices constrain future options

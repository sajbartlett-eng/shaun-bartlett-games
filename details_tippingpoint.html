THE TIPPING POINT

Profit, Policy & Planetary Collapse

4-6 Players 15-30 Minutes Semi-Cooperative Digital Hybrid

You are an industry titan. Every turn, you must invest in one of three sectors using a 2d6 rolling system. Your goal is to be the richest player, but if the group fails to save the environment, everyone loses.

The Three Markets

Success relies on rolling 2d6 against dynamic target numbers.

DEREGULATION (Target: ≤ 5)

High Risk, High Reward. Pays massively ($4-$6) but increases Crisis. If Crisis is high (5+), it accelerates (+2 Crisis). Success increases the Dereg market value, making it harder for everyone else.

CONVENTIONAL (Target: Between 5 and 9)

Safe Bet. Moderate pay ($3-$4). Still generates +1 Crisis. The safe middle ground.

GREEN TECH (Target: ≥ 9)

The Only Way Out. Low pay ($2), but increases Environment Health. Win Condition: Reach 5 Environment Health before the Crisis Limit hits.

Asymmetric Roles

Energy Sector

Industry

Heavy hitter. Huge payout ($5) for Deregulation, but high risk profile.

Mining Corp

Industry

The villain? Earns a massive $6 from Deregulation. Heavily incentivized to pollute.

Tech Startup

Industry (Special)

Ability: Breakthrough. Earns $5 from Green Tech. Success lowers the Green target, making it easier for others to help.

Agriculture

Industry

Reliable. Strong returns on Conventional markets. Less volatile.

The Finance Sector

Special Role

Does not roll dice. Instead, Finance bets on other players' success.

Predatory Lending: Can issue a VC Loan (give $1) to a poor player. The borrower takes the Debt Token. If the borrower succeeds, Finance automatically garnishes $2 from their profit.

The "Secure" Mechanic

Markets are volatile, but money buys certainty. Players can spend Wealth to shift the Target Number in their favor. Costs scale exponentially, forcing tough choices.

  • Level 0 ($0): Base Odds (e.g., Need 9+ to succeed)
  • Level 1 ($1): Improve Target by 1 (Need 8+)
  • Level 2 ($3): Improve Target by 2 (Need 7+)
  • Level 3 ($6): Improve Target by 3 (Need 6+)

Win & Loss Conditions

  • WIN Planet Saved: FIll the Environment Track to 5.
  • LOSE Collapse: The Crisis Track hits the limit (10, 12, or 14 depending on difficulty).

If the group wins, the player with the Highest Wealth is the individual winner. If the group loses, everyone fails.

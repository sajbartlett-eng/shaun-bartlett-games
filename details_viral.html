VIRAL

A 5-Minute Game of Truth vs. Attention

4-10 Players 5 Minutes Age 10+ Paper + Pen

Start with a true fact. Pass it around the table simultaneously. Each person can Pass (ignore it), Share (amplify it), or Update (rewrite it). Watch truth degrade into viral fiction under the pressure of a 45-second timer.

The Rules

Each player writes a true fact at the top of their page (the "Post"). Everyone passes their sheet to the left at the same time. When you receive a sheet, you read only the most recent entry and choose one action:

PASS (P)

You do not support this content. Three consecutive Pass actions bury (cancel) the latest Post or Update. A buried post is crossed out and cannot generate points.

SHARE (‚úì)

Amplify the content as-is. This guarantees you +1 Point, provided the Post isn't buried later by consecutive passes.

UPDATE

Rewrite the previous version to be more interesting. You can shift the tone, narrative, or perspective, but you cannot change the subject entirely. Illegal updates are treated as Buried.

Victory Condition

The game lasts for two full rotations (everyone sees every sheet twice). At the end, players total their points:

  • +1 Point for every "Share" you made on any sheet.
  • +3 Points if your original sheet is "Trending" (has the highest total shares).

What Happens

Original Post (True): "Wombat poop is cube-shaped."

Rotation 1 (Pass): Player writes "P". (Boring).

Rotation 2 (Update): Player writes: "Wombat poop is cube-shaped and used as dice by Australian gamblers."

Rotation 3-5 (Shares): Players see the amusing update and write "‚úì". The fiction goes viral.

Result: The truth was ignored. The lie scored points.

The Mechanics of Misinformation

Information Cascades

Players are only allowed to read the most recent entry on the sheet. There is no time to check the original source. You react only to what is immediately in front of you, simulating how misinformation spreads without context.

The Timer Pressure

With only 30-60 seconds per turn, fact-checking is impossible. If you don't finish writing an Update before the timer ends, it is crossed out. This pressure forces rapid, emotional reactions over critical thinking.

The Incentive Structure

To win, you need your sheet to get the most shares (Trending Bonus). This incentivizes Authors and Updaters to make content sensational rather than accurate. Truth is permissible, but only if it's interesting enough to survive.

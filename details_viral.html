Watch truth degrade into fiction in real time.

Start with a true fact. Pass it around the table. Each player can Pass (ignore it), Share (amplify it), or Update (rewrite it). Under the pressure of a 45-second timer, "boring" truth dies and sensational fiction goes viral.

The Victory Condition

The game isn't about being right; it's about being Trending. The player whose sheet gets the most "Shares" wins +3 Bonus Points. Truth is permissible, but only if it's interesting enough to survive the algorithm.

Modeling complex algorithmic bias using analog tools.

Rules Overview

The Core Loop

Each player writes a true fact at the top of their page. Everyone passes their sheet to the left simultaneously. When you receive a sheet, you read only the most recent entry and choose one action:

  • PASS (P) You do not support this content. Three consecutive Pass actions bury (cancel) the latest Post. A buried post generates zero points.
  • SHARE (‚úì) Amplify the content as-is. This guarantees you +1 Point, provided the Post isn't buried later by consecutive passes.
  • UPDATE Rewrite the previous version to be more interesting. You can shift the tone, narrative, or perspective, but you cannot change the subject entirely.

The Outcome

Original Post (True): "Wombat poop is cube-shaped."
Rotation 1 (Pass): Player writes "P". (Boring).
Rotation 2 (Update): Player writes: "Wombat poop is cube-shaped and used as dice by Australian gamblers."
Rotation 3-5 (Shares): Players see the amusing update and write "‚úì". The fiction goes viral.

BURNOUT

A Semi-Cooperative Game of Collective Pressure

üë• 2-5 Players ‚è±Ô∏è 3-5 Minutes üß† Age 10+ üé≤ Bag Building

Collaboration is mandatory. Success is individual.

You are a team working to complete a project. Everyone draws from the same shared bag. Work too hard, and you burn out. Rest too much, and the project fails.

The Corporate Dilemma

You must work hard enough to beat your teammates for the Promotion, but ensure they work hard enough to pass the Group Grade. If the group fails, the promotion is cancelled. You are trapped between individual ambition and collective survival.

The Mechanic: Rest vs. The Commons. When you Rest, you remove 2 stress: one is destroyed, but the other is returned to the shared bag [cite: 269-271].

The Insight: To save yourself, you must pollute the shared environment. Your personal relief increases the statistical probability of everyone else failing on future turns.

[cite_start]

The Mechanic: Support. You can remove a colleague's stress permanently, but only if your own stress is low (<3) [cite: 279-280].

The Insight: You cannot pour from an empty cup. The game strictly enforces the idea that empathy is a resource that requires emotional surplus.

Rules Overview

Five Actions

On your turn, you must choose one:

  • Work (Draw) Draw from the bag. Score points or accumulate Stress.
  • Rest (Pollute) Remove 2 Stress from yourself. [cite_start]One is removed permanently; the other is returned to the bag [cite: 269-271].
  • Delegate (Transfer) Move 1 Stress token from you to another player. [cite_start]Safe for the bag, dangerous for the recipient [cite: 275-277].
  • Support (Sacrifice) Remove 1 Stress token from another player and discard it permanently. [cite_start]Requires you to have low stress [cite: 279-280].
  • Overtime (Risk) Take two "Work" actions in a row. [cite_start]High efficiency, massive risk of instant burnout [cite: 282-283].

The Burnout State

If you hit 5 Stress Tokens, you Burn Out immediately.

  • The Penalty: You must discard 3 Work tokens (your score).
  • The Restriction: You lose the ability to Work or Support. You become a drain on the team until you recover.

About the Designer

Shaun Bartlett is an Asset Integrity Engineer who spent 3 years translating basketball IQ into teachable board game mechanics. He now designs experiential systems literacy tools‚Äîgames that reveal structural incentive failures across politics, economics, media, public health, and organizational dynamics.

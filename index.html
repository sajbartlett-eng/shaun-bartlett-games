The Framework

Asymmetric Interdependence

Different abilities, shared fate. The Bear hoards easily while the Rabbit struggles‚Äîbut they survive or die together. Unequal power meets mutual vulnerability.

Mechanical Honesty

The rules mirror reality: individual optimization works until the system breaks. Scoring rewards excess unless someone dies‚Äîthen it destroys you.

Cascading Consequences

Choices compound. One deregulation or one burnt-out worker affects the whole system. Events in the game ripple out just like in the real world.

Emergence Over Instruction

No lectures. You learn the lesson by failing, adapting, and experiencing the result. The game teaches you through consequences, not text.

Narrative Engines

Mechanics generate stories, not scripts. The Rabbit's death isn't predetermined‚Äîit emerges from the Fox hoarding, the Bear barricading, the Mole hiding. Every game writes its own fable.

Maximum Accessibility

Minimal components, intuitive rules, plays in minutes. Viral needs paper and pen. Leverage uses a standard deck. Digital games run in browsers. No barriers to entry.
